Frequently Asked Questions

💰 How much does a visit cost?

The initial exam starts at $89. Additional services like vaccines or diagnostics are priced à la carte.

📍 What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex. Enter your ZIP when booking to confirm availability.

🩺 What types of services do you offer?

Routine exams, vaccinations, diagnostics, minor illnesses, senior pet care, and end-of-life consultations.

🚑 Do you handle emergencies?

No, we are not an emergency service. For urgent care, we recommend a local 24/7 animal hospital.

🔄 What if I need to cancel?

You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours in advance directly through Calendly or by contacting us.
